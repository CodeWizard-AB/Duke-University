This is Duke

Duke University is a private research university in Durham, North Carolina. Founded by Methodists and Quakers in the present-day city of Trinity in 1838, the school moved to Durham in 1892.

Courses We Offer

Before you commit to a major, you should consider several factors, including the program cost, your salary expectations, and the employment rates in that field of study. In addition, you should think about your personality, your academic and professional goals, and your interests.

Engineering

Courses in engineering focus on the design, construction, and maintenance of various types of systems, from machines to buildings to software. They often involve the application of scientific principles to practical problems, and require strong problem-solving skills.

Natural Science

Courses in the natural sciences focus on the study of the physical world and the laws that govern it. They explore everything from the smallest particles that make up matter to the largest structures in the universe. Natural science courses often involve laboratory work, experimentation, and data analysis.

Humanities

Courses in the humanities focus on the study of human culture, history, and thought. They explore the creative expressions of human beings, including art, literature, music, and philosophy. Humanities courses often encourage critical thinking and analysis, and help students develop strong communication skills.

Business

Courses in business focus on the study of organizations and the management of resources, including financial resources, human resources, and marketing strategies. They often involve the application of quantitative and qualitative methods to real-world situations.
